Frequently Asked Questions

We have a whole team to support us. From design to the end of the project, we have someone special to help us through the process.
How do I get in touch?
You can get in touch with us through our social media, by email, phone call or text message.
How long does it take for my vacation home to be ready to rent?
Usually, we ask for 3 days per room and 15 days for a game room.
How long does it take for my home to be ready to move in?
It will depend on the project, but usually, we ask for 3 days per room. If we need to do any kind of woodwork, it will take a little longer.
